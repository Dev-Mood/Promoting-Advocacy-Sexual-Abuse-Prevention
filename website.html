Education and Awareness

It is important to educate people about sexual abuse and its prevention. This can be done through public awareness campaigns, school programs, and other educational initiatives.

Support Services

It is important to provide support services for victims of sexual abuse. This can include counseling, therapy, and other resources to help them heal and recover.

Reporting and Investigation

It is important to have a system in place for reporting and investigating allegations of sexual abuse. This will help to ensure that perpetrators are held accountable and that victims receive justice.
